Illusion is a videogame project I started before graduating from school. The plot is about a character who has schizophrenia. In this case Kenshio, the main character is a gamer who appears in his dreamland, Psiworld, a place full of videogame references that are in problem due to the disturbance. As we advance, we get to know that Kenshio and the disturbance are linked somehow. As we destroy the bosses of the game, we will discover that we are not only helping the towns, but we discover more of reality. Sometimes, what we think we are doing good, are just delusions or hallucinations that show that, what you thought you were doing well, was just your point of view, and you were harming some of these towns.

Alfred

This is a prototype of the interface of the webpage used by alfred. Here we can see the main menu, the recomendations for the client and the menu of the restaurant.

When we want something to eat, the user will see some of the recomendations made by the restaurant and a ML algorithm in order to accompany the selected food. Also, it is possible to see the options given to the user in order to personalize it. The last picture is the recomendation systems after the user added the food to the order.

This last two images show first, a chat between the restaurant and client in order to ask questions or favors to the staff, such as napkins or ask for the bathroom.

Alfred is my half career project for System and Computer Engineering. The project was born due to the COVID-19 pandemic, to maximize social distance. But, after asking people about it, our team realized that what we wanted was to help waiters to improve service at a restaurant. As a restaurant client, I find it unpleasant to wait more than 5 minutes for a waiter to come and take my order, it doesn't matter if I'm hurried or just wanna take my time. It is also unlikeable to have your food, and wanting something to drink, or some sauce but, because waiters take too much to attend, you finish your food or when finally someone takes your order, your food is already cold. Or, because an error happened, you must see your friends or family eat while you must wait so the restaurant can correct the order or you must conform with the wrong food. This is due to a process that may be optimized using technology. This is why Alfred wants to create a web page clients use to replace some of the waiters jobs. This includes recommendations, using an ML algorithm, personalized for every user. Also, it may be used to ask favors, or simply to see the menu, order, and pay through the app.
