Illusion is a school videogame project about a character who has schizophrenia. In this case Kenshio, the main character is a gamer who appears in his dream land, Psiworld, a place full of videogame references that is in problem due to the disturbance. As we advance, we get to know that Kenshio and the disturbance are linked somehow. As we destroy the bossess of the game, we will discover that we are not only helping the towns, but we discover more of reality. Sometimes, what we think we are doing good, are just dellusions or hallucinations that show that, what you thought you were doing well, was just your point of view, and you were actually harming some of these towns.
